Welcome

Welcome to the Vibrobeat5 website, The VB5 is an all new approach to listening and understanding music for people who are hard of hearing. No one should ever let anything stop them from pursuing what they love to do.

What is the VB5?

The VB5 is a vibrating metronome that allows users who are hard of hearing to understand the BPM of a song. This can be used by dancers, muscians, and party-goers.

Try the function of the VB5!

The VB5 can intake music and create integers for the BPM of a desired song. The integers can then be sent to the VB5 prototype and create vibrations for the user to understand. This website is specifically meant to show the functionality of the beginning steps, below is a valid representation.
